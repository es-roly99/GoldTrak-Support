# Privacy Policy for GoldTrak

**Last Updated**: November 2024

## Introduction

GoldTrak ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our jewelry inventory management application.

### What We Collect

**Locally Stored Data (On Your Device)**:
- Jewelry inventory information (descriptions, prices, weights, images)
- Transaction history (sales and purchases)
- Business settings (store name, currency, language preferences)
- Custom categories and configurations

**Optional Data Sent to Third Parties**:
- Service request form submissions (when you request enterprise features):
  - Your name
  - Email address
  - Phone number (optional)
  - Service description

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Data Usage
All inventory and transaction data remains on your device and is used solely for:
- Displaying your jewelry inventory
- Calculating statistics and analytics
- Generating reports and exports
- Managing sales and transactions

### Service Request Data
When you submit a service request for enterprise features:
- Data is sent via Web3Forms (email relay service) to our team
- Used only to respond to your inquiry
- Temporarily stored by Web3Forms for delivery verification (30-90 days)
- NOT sold, shared, or used for marketing purposes

**Web3Forms Privacy Policy**: https://web3forms.com/privacy

### Gold Price Data
- We fetch real-time gold prices from public market data APIs
- No personal information is sent when retrieving price data
- Price data is used solely for display within the app

## Third-Party Services

### Web3Forms (Email Service)
- **Purpose**: Delivers service request form submissions to our team
- **Data Shared**: Name, email, phone, service description
- **Privacy Policy**: https://web3forms.com/privacy
- **Data Retention**: 30-90 days for delivery verification
- **GDPR Compliant**: Yes

### Gold Price APIs
- **Purpose**: Fetch real-time gold market prices
- **Data Shared**: None (anonymous requests)
- **No Personal Information**: Price data fetched anonymously

## Data Security

### On-Device Security
- All inventory data stored locally using secure device storage
- Photos stored in app's private directory
- No data transmitted to external servers (except service requests)

### Transmission Security
- Service requests sent via HTTPS encrypted connection
- Email relay uses industry-standard security practices
